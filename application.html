If you would like to insure your pet not only against operations, but also for all outpatient and inpatient treatments without surgery in the event of an accident or illness, we recommend the following additional module.

Medical treatment and preventive care protection

ⓘ
23.38 € per month

Medical treatment coverage covers outpatient and inpatient treatments without surgery (medical treatments) in the event of an accident or illness. We cover the costs of treatments for conditions such as allergies, stomach problems, bite injuries, or sprains. Additionally, a subsidy is included for the following routine and preventative services: vaccinations, deworming, tick control, and tooth extractions.
